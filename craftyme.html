About Project

  • CraftyMe is an iOS and Android app that allows you to keep track of all of your artwork in one place.
  • Everyone produces a great deal of creative art, craft, drawings, and paintings.
  • We usually keep these memories in drawing books or scrapbooks.
  • This app will assist users in converting their physical artwork to digital format.
